About Pony Wings

Pony Wings is an exhilarating rollerscooting adventure that captures the spirit of perseverance and the joy of flight. In this journey, you guide Scootaloo - a young filly with big dreams - as she uses the topography of the land to achieve heights her tiny wings couldn't reach alone.
